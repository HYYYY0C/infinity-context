### **3. 测试配置** ✅

**文件**: `pyproject.toml`

**配置工具**:
- **pytest**: 测试运行器
- **coverage**: 代码覆盖率
- **black**: 代码格式化
- **mypy**: 类型检查

**配置示例**:
```toml
[tool.pytest.ini_options]
testpaths = ["tests"]
python_files = "test_*.py"
addopts = "-v --tb=short"

[tool.black]
line-length = 120
target-version = ['py38']
```
